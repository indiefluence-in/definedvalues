Defined Values Trainers Network Summit 2022

Defined Values Trainers Network Summit 2022

We aim to induct each citizen of this planet into the vision of Project YugParivartan for which we have designed various trainings and events. DVTN conducts regular knowledge sharing seminars and summits to enable knowledge flow and increase learning agility in citizens of this planet. DVTN Summit 2022 is a spectacular event to bring forward the talent of Trainers aggregated with DVTN by giving them an opportunity to speak on the subjects relevant to humanity.


About the Organizers

The Defined Values Trainers Network Summit 2022 is organized by Defined Values Trainers Network (DVTN), a vertical of Project YugParivartan Synergy Platform which is a wholly owned NGO arm of Defined Values Consultants Pvt Ltd.


Flow of Event
  • 4:00 pm to 4:15 pm - Agenda Setting and DVTN Presentation by Mr. Pradeep Malhotra, Founder President of Defined Values Trainers Network
  • 4:15 pm to 4:40 pm - Session by Ms. Sudha Bonsor on "Relationships - Building Blocks of the Society"
  • 4:45 pm to 5:10 pm - Session by Ms. Nirupama Khare on "Trust and Leadership"
  • 5:15 pm to 5:40 pm - Session by Dr. Vansh Raj Maurya on "Food Body Mind"
  • 5:45 pm to 6:10 pm - Session by Mr. Venkat Iyer on "Know Your Financial Health"
  • 6:15 pm to 6:40 pm - Session by Ms. Anita Budhraja on "Beautify Life Be WELLTHY"
  • 6:45 pm to 7:10 pm - Session by Mr. C Mani on "Work for total community development"
  • 7:15 pm to 7:40 pm - Session by Ms. Garima Verma on "Sustainability"
  • 7:40 pm to 7:55 pm - Q & A and Panel Discussion with all Speakers Moderated by Chief Mentor Hitesh Chandel
Event Details
  • Date: December 6, 2022
  • Time: 4:00 pm - 8:00 pm
