Vision, Mission, and Objectives


Code of Citizen’s Conduct (Rashtradharma)

You can read the first few chapters of the book on Amazon Kindle or Author's Blog free of cost before deciding to order a Paperback or eBook edition. This book contains 460 high-quality pages and 184 pictures of the design framework on which our creator has created all of us and the entire Ecosystem.


About the Book

This book serves as a universal user manual, explaining the design framework of our creator and detailing the maximum potential of each human being.

It unifies religions, faith systems, and leadership philosophies into a Utopian theory, summarizing humanity's past, present, and future, and offering solutions for global issues like inflation, corruption, and Global Warming through self-actualized leadership.

It aims to bring together all mentors to effectively share the highest wisdom, addressing the real needs of humanity and ensuring their fulfillment. In short, it provides a vision to make Earth the best place to live and work.

Finally, it invites readers to join “Project YugParivartan”, a grand change event, to discover their role and make history.


Chapter by Chapter Summary

The content is structured into 26 Articles (Chapters), each building on the previous to provide a clear vision to fix issues like corruption, inflation, recession, pollution, unemployment, poverty, terrorism, Global Warming, and other vices confronting humanity.

  • Article 0.0 - Preface: Summarizes the intent behind the book, which aims to revive living conditions and ensure a safe planet for future generations.
  • Article 0.1 - Mission and Vision: Focuses on developing the planet as the best place to live and work, restoring Bharat's status as Vishwaguru.
  • Article 0.2 - Need for Change: Explains the need for divine intervention and the author's realization to become the change he wants to see.
  • Articles 0.3 to 0.6: Depicts dialogues between the author and souls of different eras, exploring the collective human consciousness.
  • Article 0.7 - Project YugParivartan: Proposes the author’s involvement in the Grand Change process.
  • Article 0.8 - God’s Mental Map: Explains how aligning with God’s mental map can lead to divine manifestations.
  • Article 0.9 - Change Agent Network: Discusses the roles of change agents in the Grand Change.
  • Article 0.10 - Needs of a Human Being: Explains the Maslow Model of Human Needs and the scope of Project YugParivartan.
  • Article 0.11 - Points of View: Details the creation of different viewpoints.
  • Article 0.12 - Why Yoga?: Shows how yoga can align viewpoints with God's.
  • Article 0.13 - Ten Elements of All Creation: Discusses the material and conscious elements of the universe.
  • Article 0.14 - Destiny of a Soul: Emphasizes aligning actions with the correct destiny.
  • Article 0.15 - Science and Spirituality: Explains the science and spirituality of human existence.
  • Article 0.16 - Chaturyugi Cycle: Details the creation cycle of four eras.
  • Article 0.17 - Defined Values: Introduces the highest order value system established by God.
  • Article 0.18 - Four Levels of God Connection: Explains the connection levels based on consciousness evolution.
  • Article 0.19 - Four Pillars of Humanity: Discusses the four eternal pillars of institutions and the need to remove unnecessary divisions.
  • Article 0.20 - Cause and Effect: Explains the root cause of vices and their effects on the planet.
  • Article 0.21 - Evidence of the Last Prophet: Reveals God's manifestation to elevate human consciousness.
  • Article 0.22 - New World Order – Back to the Oldest One: Describes the design of Vedic Cities to save humanity.
  • Article 0.23 - Guru Shishya Parampara: Explains the methodology for imparting wisdom.
  • Article 0.24 - Chief Yog Officer: Provides evidence of God’s human manifestation and the author's role.
  • Article 0.25 - Acknowledgements, Apologies and Invitations: Reflects on the author’s realization, acknowledgements, and invitations to readers to join Project YugParivartan.

About the Author

Hitesh Chandel, born in New Delhi, worked in IT as a programmer, Technical Lead, and Project Manager. His career took him to the US, Germany, Italy, and Singapore, but his passion for contributing to his Motherland led him to study ancient Vedic philosophy. Achieving spiritual enlightenment at 35, he founded Defined Values Consultants to provide leadership and management consulting based on Vedic wisdom. This book, a result of his deep meditation, addresses issues like corruption, poverty, over-population, recession, and Global Warming.

He launched “Project YugParivartanTM” (Project Grand Change) to realize this vision through the Self-Actualized Leadership Development ProgramTM.
