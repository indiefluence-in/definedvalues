Vision, Mission, and Objectives

Defined Values Consultants Pvt Ltd is integrating individuals and institutions into a Global Collaboration Framework to address the root causes of major ecosystem problems, creating a win-win situation for all citizens of the planet. Codenamed Project YugParivartan (Project Grand Change), we conduct regular public events to interface with the target audience, explaining our vision, mission, framework, benefits, and expectations, enabling informed decisions for professional and personal development. Registration forms for upcoming events are published on our website and other event listing websites from time to time.


Professional Services as part of Project YugParivartan


1. Self-Actualized Leadership Network (SALN) is a group of specially trained leaders whose final mission is registered with SALN. This Mission Networking and lifelong learning platform for leaders conducts regular seminars to explain the vision of Project YugParivartan to leadership aspirants and spiritual seekers.

Fill Form 1 below to register for the upcoming "Self-Actualized Leadership Development Seminar".

2. Defined Values Trainers Network (DVTN) is a trusted global organized network setup to aggregate high-quality trainers required to channelize the potential of an organization’s workforce into high performance. We aim to induct each citizen into the vision of Project YugParivartan through various communications and trainings. All trainers are trained through SALDP and conduct regular knowledge-sharing seminars.

3. Defined Values Business Network (DVBN) is a trusted organized network of businesspersons and professionals from various domains. Call 9810375146 to know more about DVBN and become part of this rapidly growing community of high-conscious business persons.

4. Self-Realized Mentorship Network (SRMN) is a group of SRMDP certified professionals who deliver high-quality SALDP content to participants. SRMDP, or Self-Realized Mentorship Development Program, creates self-realized mentors. Those wishing to become spiritual gurus can first attend the SALN seminar before a 1-to-1 meeting with our Chief Mentor, Hitesh Chandel. Email hitesh.chandel@definedvalues.org with the subject "1-to-1 request for SRMN".

Vision Statement

“To be a global institution of self-realized mentors from all religious, social, political, and regional backgrounds, facilitating the unified transition of the highest order wisdom to each citizen of this planet.”


Mission Statement

“To develop and network self-actualized change leaders from all walks of life to fix the root causes of issues adversely affecting the quality of life of citizens on this planet, by creating a Global Integrated Ecosystem of peace, prosperity, justice, and universal harmony.”


Company Objectives

  • To provide high-value leadership training to corporate employees and professionals to improve their management and leadership effectiveness.
  • To guide citizens to actualize their full potential by aligning them with values prescribed by great leaders, philosophers, and self-realized souls.
  • To facilitate the realization of the Mission Statement of participants on a best-effort basis, aligned with the objectives of Project YugParivartan.
