About Self-Actualized Leadership Development Program

06 Dec 2022

About Self-Actualized Leadership Development Program

Self-Actualization allows individuals to make highly effective decisions and become visionary leaders. Our Founder and Chief Mentor, Mr. Hitesh Chandel, has researched extensively to create a vision documented in “Code of Citizen’s Conduct,” which has earned him the “Pride of India Award.”

SALDP is our flagship program designed to develop participants' full potential, substantially improving their personal and professional lives through a clearly laid out plan. It provides eternal principles of effective leadership and networking platforms for collaborative growth.

Developing Highly Effective Organizations THROUGH SALDP

An organization is a group of individuals working together to achieve a shared vision. Setting up a universal value system is crucial for maintaining integrity. SALDP aligns individuals and institutions with eternally defined values, fostering leadership and effective resource utilization.

Key Benefits of SALDP

For Companies:

  • More Leadership - Less Management: Develops participants into thinkers, improving service quality.
  • Enhanced Will to Work: Aligns thought processes with eternal principles, boosting productivity.
  • Personal Effectiveness: Increases influence on others, reducing collaboration roadblocks.
  • Reduced Attrition: Keeps workforce motivated under self-actualizing leaders.
  • Networking Opportunities: Opens new business and career paths through Vedic Wisdom Group.
  • Access to Trained Resource Pool: Provides a high energy resource pool for organizations.
  • Improvised Ecosystem: Reduces negative effects of ecosystem issues on business.

For Individuals:

  • Smooth Transition to Employer: Addresses early stage entrepreneurial challenges.
  • Networking with Professionals: Opens opportunities for clients, partners, and resources.
  • Leadership Opportunities: Offers leadership roles within the SALDP network.
  • Improved Personal and Professional Life: Enhances understanding of the world, improving quality of life.
  • Spiritual Growth: Provides a path to spiritual awakening and liberation.
